About Us

At Voltair, we address the unique challenges of the drone industry with cutting-edge solutions. Our LiPo Battery Management System transforms conventional batteries into smarter ones by assigning them unique identities and digitizing their data.

 

We are deeply committed to environmental sustainability, tackling the issue of improper battery disposal, which poses significant ecological risks. By promoting safe disposal practices, Voltair is paving the way for a greener future

Our system enhances battery lifespan, minimizes waste, and provides intelligent management solutions. Additionally, we are developing a range of products aimed at digitizing everyday operations for both private and public drone companies, helping to streamline and safeguard their activities.

Our Mission

The drone industry in India is still emerging and largely untapped, with many drone pilots and ground teams facing unique challenges due to a lack of effective market solutions. At Voltair, we are committed to creating innovative products that address these gaps.

 

Our mission is to empower and support every drone company in India by providing tailored solutions that drive innovation, improve efficiency, and enhance safety. Through our work, we aim to foster sustainable growth in the drone industry, helping businesses overcome obstacles and succeed in this rapidly evolving sector.

VOLITOR 6s

Our unique product revolutionizes 6S LiPo battery monitoring and analysis with its all-in-one solution. It provides real-time tracking of total and individual cell voltages, paired with automated hourly recording for up to a month without user intervention.

An integrated app displays detailed charts for advanced analytics and supports seamless data export, ideal for R&D purposes.

Designed for precision and ease of use, this device replaces traditional LiPo checkers and multimeters, simplifying field operations and everyday tasks.

With its innovative features, it enhances efficiency and reliability, making it the ultimate tool for drone battery management and performance optimization.
Features:
Total Voltage of Battery
Voltage of Each cells
Lipo/ Li-ion battery support.

VOLITOR 6s+

Our product is an advanced solution for monitoring 6S LiPo drone batteries, offering enhanced features over the Volitor 6S Basic Chip.

It provides real-time tracking of total voltage, individual cell voltages, temperature, and altitude (atmospheric pressure), delivering valuable insights for drone OEMs and battery management teams.

With automated hourly recording for up to three months, it ensures accurate and reliable data storage without user intervention. Data can be easily exported in PDF, CSV, or XLS formats and shared via WhatsApp or email.

Built for precision, reliability, and convenience, this tool optimizes battery performance and enhances drone safety and efficiency.
Features:
Total Voltage of Battery
View and interact with Histogram
3 month storage
Temp and Barometer Sensor
Export to PDF, CSV, Xls
Voltage of Each cells.
