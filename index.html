Our Mission

At Voltair, we're on a mission to revolutionize the world of batteries monitoring. Our Lipo Battery Management System is designed to make batteries smarter and give an identity, utilizing the latest technology to achieve our goals.

We are committed to promote environmental responsibility by ensuring the battery telemetry is balanced until there is no point of recovery. we will then collect the battery and do a safe disposal of batteries, contributing to a greener and more sustainable future for all.

With a focus on eco-friendly practices, our Battery Management System optimizes battery lifespan, reducing the environmental impact of battery waste, managing and organizing multiple battery smartly.

FAQ

How does the device works?

Its a Device which gives each battery an identity and Monitors it with our Voltair app.

Is it user-friendly?

Absolutely! We can manage multiple batteries with this app, Userinterface is very easy to use and very userfriendly for even a person in remote area to upload battery report to the Drone OEM.

When can we get Voltair pod for 6s?

We will be making a budget friendly 6s Voltair pod by year end.

Is it really working on eco-friendly disposal of battery?

Definitely! We are working our way to make sure the battery is safe for disposal rather then people digging it below the ground rather then sending it to the E-waste disposal center in India.

Is it compatible with all batteries?

Our system is compatible with LIPO batteries and Li Ion batteries for 12s and 6s capacities.
